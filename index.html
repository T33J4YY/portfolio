About

I'm an AI Product Designer with strong UX and UI fundamentals and hands-on experience designing conversational, LLM-powered experiences. I shape prompts, flows, states, and interaction patterns for interfaces that sit on top of language models — and feel natural, on-brand, and genuinely useful.

I work closely with engineers to understand model behaviour and design interfaces that align with its strengths and limitations. I'm comfortable across discovery, research, low and high-fidelity prototyping, and workshops with stakeholders. I'm excited by where design is heading and keen to help shape what modern, AI-powered experiences look and feel like.
