## How to export from Figma

1. Select the frame or group you want to export
2. In the right panel, scroll to **Export**
3. Add export setting: **PNG** (or JPG), 2x for retina
4. Click **Export [name]**
5. Rename the file to match the table above and move it into `portfolio/images/`

Recommended size: 1200–1600px wide for good quality on screen.
